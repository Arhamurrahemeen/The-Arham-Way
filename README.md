## The Evolution of the Method
1. **[span_3](start_span)The Code-First Trap:** Jumping straight into VS Code leads to directionless development[span_3](end_span).
2. **[span_4](start_span)The Design-First Limitation:** Designing UI without clear goals leads to backend headaches and scope creep[span_4](end_span).
3. **[span_5](start_span)The Spec-Driven Standard:** Documenting goals, scope, and technical specs *before* designing or coding[span_5](end_span).

## The AI Orchestration Stack
* **[span_6](start_span)Blueprint & Architecture:** **Claude** – Used for deep research, flow patterns, and high-fidelity technical documentation[span_6](end_span).
* **[span_7](start_span)UI/UX Generation:** **Google Stitch** – Transforming documented specs into beautiful front-end layouts with precision prompting[span_7](end_span).
* **Integration:** **Gemini & AI Studio** – For debugging and complex logic handoffs.
